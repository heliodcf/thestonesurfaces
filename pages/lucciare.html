THE LUCCIARE STORY

A Decade of Innovation, Born in Italy

Lucciare surfaces were designed in Italy and developed over 10 years before the brand's establishment. The company created Polarstone® in 2013 to introduce Calacatta and Statuario quartz designs to the market, then launched the Lucciare® brand in 2019 with refined manufacturing processes.

Today, Lucciare represents the latest evolution in countertop surfaces — combining beautiful handcrafted designs with engineered durability. Their intricate veining patterns replicate natural marble while offering superior stain resistance and low maintenance.

WHY LUCCIARE

The Best of All Worlds

Italian Design

Every surface is conceived in Italy, where centuries of stone artistry meet modern engineering. The result is handcrafted veining that captures the soul of natural marble.

Stain Resistant

Non-porous and microbial-free, Lucciare surfaces resist stains, bacteria, and everyday wear without sealing. Engineered for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic spaces.

Certified Safe

Triple-certified with GreenGuard Gold, GreenGuard, and NSF standards. Guaranteed safe for indoor air quality and food contact surfaces in homes and commercial spaces.

Exceptional Durability

Engineered quartz provides superior resilience against scratches, chips, and heat. Low-maintenance surfaces that maintain their beauty for years without reconditioning.
