OUR COLLECTIONS

Explore 500+ Stone Slab Varieties

Granite

One of the hardest natural stones available. Granite offers unmatched durability and timeless appeal, available in hundreds of color variations from classic blacks and whites to exotic movements.

Marble

Timeless elegance refined over millennia. Our marble selection spans Italian classics, Spanish varieties, and exotic imports — from pure whites to dramatic veined statement pieces.

Quartz

Engineered for modern living. Our quartz collection includes exclusive brands Hanstone by Hyundai L&C and Lucciare Handcrafted Quartz — non-porous, virtually maintenance-free surfaces with exceptional consistency.

Quartzite

Nature's hardest metamorphic stone. Quartzite combines the beauty of marble with the durability of granite, making it the preferred choice for high-use kitchen countertops and outdoor applications.

Porcelain

Ultra-thin, ultra-strong. Large-format porcelain panels deliver a seamless aesthetic for flooring, wall cladding, and furniture surfaces — resistant to heat, stains, and UV fading.

Dolomite & Hard Marble

The sophisticated middle ground between marble and quartzite. Dolomite offers the delicate veining of marble with significantly enhanced hardness, ideal for clients who want luxury without high-maintenance.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Orlando Showroom Questions Answered

Where is the Orlando showroom?

We're at 3924 N John Young Pkwy, Orlando, FL 32804 — easily accessible from I-4, the 408 (East-West Expressway), and the Florida Turnpike. Free parking available on-site.

Do you serve Daytona Beach and Melbourne?

Yes. Our Orlando location serves all of Central Florida including Volusia County (Daytona Beach), Brevard County (Melbourne, Cape Canaveral), and everywhere in between. We offer delivery throughout the region.

How does Orlando's selection compare to Miami?

Our Orlando showroom carries the same 500+ slab varieties and exclusive brands (Hanstone Quartz, Lucciare) as our Miami flagship. If you see something online or in our Miami showroom, our Orlando team can source it for you.

Can contractors set up a trade account?

Absolutely. We work with fabricators, contractors, designers, and architects throughout Central Florida. Contact us to discuss trade pricing, priority access to new inventory, and dedicated account support.

Is the Orlando showroom open on weekends?

Yes, we're open Saturday from 9am to 1pm. Weekday hours are Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm. We're closed on Sundays. No appointment needed — walk in anytime.

Ready to Visit Our Orlando Showroom?

Walk in anytime during business hours — no appointment needed. Our team is ready to help you find the perfect stone for your project.
