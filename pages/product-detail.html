Technical Specifications

Alaska White Granite is a natural igneous rock composed primarily of feldspar, quartz, and mica. Its distinctive white and grey patterning with subtle flecks of garnet and biotite makes each slab a unique work of nature. Quarried from premium deposits in Brazil.

6–7 on the Mohs scale. This makes Alaska White Granite highly resistant to scratching and abrasion, suitable for high-traffic areas and heavy daily use in kitchens and commercial spaces.

< 0.4% — Alaska White Granite has a very low water absorption rate, making it an excellent choice for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, and other areas exposed to moisture. Sealing is recommended annually to maintain optimal performance.

14.5 MPa (ASTM C880). Provides excellent structural integrity for countertop overhangs, island installations, and commercial applications. Supports standard overhang depths without additional reinforcement when properly installed.

  • Kitchen countertops & islands
  • Bathroom vanities & shower surrounds
  • Fireplace surrounds
  • Outdoor kitchens (with proper sealing)
  • Commercial bar tops & reception desks
  • Feature walls & accent surfaces
