Hyundai L&C Official Partner

Created by Earth. Designed for You.

Born from a partnership between Hyundai L&C and the finest raw materials on earth, Hanstone Quartz has been manufactured in London, Ontario since 2009. Every slab is crafted using advanced Breton™ technology — the gold standard in engineered stone production.

OUR HERITAGE

A Legacy of Innovation, Rooted in Nature

Hanstone Quartz represents the perfect marriage of natural beauty and modern engineering. Composed of 93% natural quartz — one of the hardest minerals on earth — every surface delivers the organic warmth of natural stone with the reliability of cutting-edge manufacturing.

Produced at Hyundai L&C's state-of-the-art facility in Canada, Hanstone is committed to sustainable production. From responsible sourcing of raw materials to energy-efficient manufacturing processes, every slab reflects a commitment to both quality and the environment.

  • ✓93% natural quartz for superior hardness and durability
  • ✓Canadian-manufactured with Breton™ compaction technology
  • ✓Greenguard Gold Certified for low chemical emissions
  • ✓Backed by Hyundai L&C's global quality standards

WHY HANSTONE

Engineered to Outperform

Harder Than Granite

Composed of 93% natural quartz, Hanstone scores a 7 on the Mohs hardness scale — making it one of the most durable countertop surfaces available. It resists scratching, chipping, and cracking under everyday use.

Never Needs Sealing

Unlike natural stone, Hanstone Quartz never requires sealing, polishing, or reconditioning. The non-porous surface maintains its luster and finish for the lifetime of your countertop with minimal maintenance.

Non-Porous & Hygienic

Hanstone's non-porous surface means no bacteria, mold, or mildew can penetrate the material. Greenguard Gold Certified for indoor air quality, it's the ideal choice for kitchens and bathrooms.

Lifetime Warranty

Every Hanstone slab is backed by a transferable lifetime residential warranty and a 10-year commercial warranty. It's a promise of quality from a global manufacturer you can trust.
