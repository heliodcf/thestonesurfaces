ABOUT THE STONE SURFACES

Our Story

A decade of excellence in natural and engineered stone.

A DECADE OF GROWTH

Our Journey

2016

Founded in Doral

Started with a small warehouse and a vision to bring the finest natural stone to South Florida's builders and homeowners.

2018

Expanded Our Showroom

Opened our 10,000 sq ft showroom, giving clients the ability to see and touch hundreds of slabs before making a decision.

2020

Great Selection on Display

Became the largest stone selection in South Florida, curating premium materials from quarries around the world.

2022

Trade Program Launch

Launched dedicated services for fabricators, designers, and contractors with wholesale pricing and priority access.

2024

Regional Growth

Expanded our delivery network to serve fabricators and builders across the entire state of Florida.

2025

Lucciare Partnership

Became a Lucciare Handcrafted Quartz distributor, adding Italian-designed, GreenGuard Gold certified surfaces to our premium collection.

2026

Hanstone Partnership

Became the official Hanstone Quartz distributor by Hyundai L&C, bringing world-class engineered surfaces to our clients.

WHAT DRIVES US

Mission & Values

Every slab we source, every client we serve, and every project we support is guided by three foundational pillars.

♦

Quality

We hand-select every slab from trusted quarries worldwide. No shortcuts, no compromises. Every surface that leaves our warehouse meets the highest standards for color, consistency, and structural integrity.

★

Service

From your first showroom visit to final delivery, our team provides expert guidance at every step. We answer the phone, we follow through, and we treat your project like our own.

◆

Integrity

Transparent pricing, honest timelines, and straightforward advice. We build long-term relationships by doing what we say we'll do, every single time.
