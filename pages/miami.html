OUR MAIN SHOWROOM

South Florida's Premier Stone Destination

Located in the heart of Doral — South Florida's thriving business corridor — our Miami showroom is the flagship location of The Stone Surfaces. As direct importers, we maintain one of the largest selections of natural and engineered stone slabs in the region, with over 500 varieties available for immediate viewing.

Whether you're a homeowner planning a kitchen renovation, an interior designer sourcing materials for a luxury project, or a fabricator looking for reliable wholesale supply, our Miami location has the selection and expertise to match your vision. Our team speaks English, Spanish, and Portuguese, making it easy for South Florida's diverse community to get the guidance they need.

The Doral showroom also serves as our distribution hub, with direct access to Miami's international ports — which means fresh inventory, competitive pricing, and materials you won't find anywhere else in the region.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About Our Miami Showroom

Where exactly is the Miami showroom?

Our Miami showroom is located at 7972 NW 56th St in Doral, FL 33166 — right in the heart of Doral's business district. We're easily accessible from the Palmetto Expressway (SR 826) and the Dolphin Expressway (SR 836). Free parking is available on-site.

Do you serve the Florida Keys from Miami?

Yes. We deliver to the Florida Keys and all of Monroe County. Many of our Keys-based clients visit our Doral showroom to view slabs in person and arrange delivery to their project site.

Can I bring my interior designer to the showroom?

Absolutely. We welcome designers, architects, and contractors. Many design professionals visit with their clients to help select the perfect material. Our team can also provide spec sheets and samples for your design presentations.

Do you offer delivery in South Florida?

Yes. We deliver throughout Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We also offer statewide Florida delivery and international shipping to the Caribbean, including Puerto Rico and the Bahamas.

What languages do you speak at the Miami showroom?

Our Miami team is fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese — reflecting the diverse community we serve in South Florida.

Ready to Visit Our Miami Showroom?

Walk in anytime during business hours — no appointment needed. Our expert team is ready to help you find the perfect stone.
